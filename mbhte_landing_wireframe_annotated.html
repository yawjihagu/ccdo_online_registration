Section 1
Navbar — Fixed Navigation Bar
Stays visible as the user scrolls. Contains the MBHTE logo + center name on the left, and five navigation links (Home, Programs, Locations, Schedule, Login) on the right. Collapses to a hamburger menu on mobile. Becomes fully opaque on scroll.
Section 2
Hero — Center Showcase
First impression. A gold scholarship badge anchors the top, followed by the center name as the main headline. A subtitle gives the location. Three CTA buttons serve all audiences: Enroll Now (new applicants), Browse Programs (explorers), and Track Application (returning users). Four stat boxes highlight key figures. Animated waves flow at the bottom for visual energy.
Section 3
Sectors — Program Categories
6 clickable tiles group all 15 programs by industry sector: ICT, Tourism & Food, Construction, Health, Agriculture, and Security. Each tile shows an icon, sector name, and program count. Clicking scrolls down to the Programs section. Helps users quickly find courses relevant to their field.
Section 4
Why Us — Value Proposition
3 benefit cards on the green background address the most common questions from applicants and partner institutions: Is it free? (100% scholarship), Is it recognized? (TESDA NC certificate), and Is the training quality? (accredited trainers & facilities). Builds trust before the user reaches the program list.
Section 5
Quick Access — Action Hub
4 shortcut cards for the most common tasks: Online Registration, Track Application, Find Locations, and Training Schedule. Designed for returning visitors who know what they need and want to get there fast — without scrolling through the full page.
Section 6
Programs — Course Catalog
A 3-column grid of 15 program cards, each showing a sector icon, course name, training hours, schedule, location, benefits, and a Pre-Register button. Cards are organized alphabetically. The consistent card layout makes it easy to scan and compare. Each card links directly to the registration modal.
Section 7
Map — Training Center Locator
An embedded interactive map (loaded via iframe from ccdomap.html) showing TESDA-accredited training centers across BARMM. Helps general public and walk-in visitors find the nearest center. Especially useful for users from provinces outside Cotabato City.
Section 8
Calendar — Schedule & Announcements
Two-column layout: left column lists upcoming training schedules with dates, titles, and venue details. Right column shows important announcements like scholarship application periods, walk-in registration info, and new center openings. Keeps all audiences — applicants, partners, and the general public — informed without needing to contact the office.
Section 9
Footer — Contact & Navigation
4-column layout: About (logo + agency description + social links), Quick Links (repeat of key navigation for accessibility), Contact Us (phone, email, address), and Office Hours (with a "Limited Slots" notice). A copyright bar closes the page. The logo in the footer reinforces brand identity and institutional credibility.
